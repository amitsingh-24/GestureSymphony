GestureSymphony – Conduct a full symphony using only your hands

Control your local music with simple hand gestures.

Powered by MediaPipe Hands, OpenCV, and pygame, this demo lets you play, pause, skip tracks, adjust volume, and more.

How to Use

Follow these steps to get started:

Step 1

Allow webcam access and ensure good lighting with a simple background.

Step 2

Use these gestures:

  • 1 finger → Pause
  • 2 fingers → Play
  • 3 fingers → Next Track
Step 3

Click "Capture Frame" to process your gesture and control the music.
